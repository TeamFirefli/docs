### Requesting a Notice
<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h4>Open the Notices tab</h4>
    <p>Navigate to the Notices section in your workspace sidebar.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Click "New Notice"</h4>
    <p>Fill in the notice details including type, dates, and reason.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Submit for approval</h4>
    <p>Your request will be sent to your workspace administrators.</p>
  </Step>
</Steps>
