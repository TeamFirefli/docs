## Overview
Orbit tracks Roblox activity by reading from your exported session logs and syncing them to the workspace timeline. Follow these steps to connect your game with the monitoring pipeline.

  <h3 className="!mt-0">Configure the Activity panel</h3>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h4>Open Settings</h4>
    <p>Open **Settings â†’ Activity** from the workspace sidebar.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Select minimum rank</h4>
    <p>Select the Roblox group role that represents the lowest rank you wish to monitor.</p>
    <Note>
      Selecting a role includes all ranks that are equal to or higher than it in the tracking window.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<div className="flex items-center gap-2 mb-4">
  <span className="inline-flex h-8 w-8 items-center justify-center rounded-full bg-slate-800 text-sm font-semibold text-slate-100">
    2
  </span>
  <h3 className="!mt-0">Import the Orbit Loader</h3>
</div>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h4>Download the loader</h4>
    <p>Download the latest loader from the **Activity** panel.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Insert into Roblox Studio</h4>
    <p>In Roblox Studio, right-click **ServerScriptService â†’ Insert from File** and choose the loader.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Configure the script</h4>
    <p>Locate the generated `OrbitActivity` script and open the `Configuration` section. Update fields such as `minutesTillAFK`, `minTrackedRank`, `groupId`, `rankChecking`, `cooldownPeriod`, and `bansEnabled`.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Publish your place</h4>
    <p>Save and publish your place.</p>
    <Warning>
      Roblox requires HTTPS for outgoing requests. Enable HTTPS in Studio before saving the script. Need help? [Roblox HTTPS docs](https://create.roblox.com/docs/security/https-requests).
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>
