### Open Cloud
<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h4>Create OAuth Application</h4>
    <p>You'll require an active Open Cloud OAuth Application to link with Orbit. Create an application [here](https://create.roblox.com/dashboard/credentials?activeTab=OAuthTab).</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Name your application</h4>
    <p>Name the application name something unique. This will be displayed to everyone that uses it so pick wisely!</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Save credentials</h4>
    <p>Save both Client ID and Secret (You'll need this later).</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Fill out details</h4>
    <p>Fill out the relevant details, you can use our Policy links. Replace 'instance' with your unique Instance name.</p>

```
Entry Link:
https://instance.planetaryapp.cloud/login

Privacy Policy:
https://www.planetaryapp.us/privacy

Terms of Service:
https://www.planetaryapp.us/terms
```
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Select scopes</h4>
    <p>In the scopes section, select **OpenID** and **Profile**.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Setup redirect URL</h4>
    <p>Setup a redirect URL. You can find this on the home page of your Instance (Blue settings Icon).</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Review and Publish</h4>
    <p>Press Review and Publish! This will trigger Roblox moderation to test your signin page.</p>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Orbit Settings
<Steps>
  <Step>
    <h4>Enter credentials</h4>
    <p>In the same section as your unique redirect URL, you'll see two input boxes. Fill out your **Client ID** and **Secret** that you saved earlier, and click save.</p>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    <h4>Complete!</h4>
    <p>Orbit will now show a Signin / Signup with Roblox button on your Login page!</p>
  </Step>
</Steps>
